PARIS

Paris, the capital of France, is renowned for its rich history, stunning architecture, and cultural significance.Iconic landmarks such as the Eiffel Tower, the Louvre Museum, and Notre-Dame Cathedral are among the city's most recognized attractions. Paris is also famous for its charming neighborhoods, like Montmartre and Le Marais, which showcase a blend of historical elegance and modern vibrancy. With its tree-lined boulevards, world-class museums, and elegant boutiques, Paris continues to captivate visitors with its timeless beauty and cultural heritage.
