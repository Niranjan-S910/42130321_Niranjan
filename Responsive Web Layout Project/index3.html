LONDON

London, the capital of the United Kingdom, is a dynamic city known for its rich history, cultural diversity, and iconic landmarks. From the historic Tower of London and Buckingham Palace to the modern London Eye and the Shard, the city effortlessly blends the old with the new. London is a global center for finance, fashion,and entertainment, with world-renowned theaters in the West End, prestigious museums like the British Museum,and vibrant neighborhoods such as Soho and Covent Garden.London's diverse culinary scene and eclectic mix of cultures make it a truly cosmopolitan destination.
